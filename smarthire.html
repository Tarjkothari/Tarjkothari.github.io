About the Project

SmartHire is an innovative recruitment platform designed to solve the inefficiency of manual resume screening. Similar to major job boards like Naukri.com, it allows job providers to list vacancies, but adds a layer of AI intelligence.

Key Features
  • AI Resume Ranking: The system analyzes candidate resumes against the job description.
  • Validity Scoring: Recruiters receive a "hire validity" score to instantly gauge candidate fit.
  • Job Listing Management: seamless interface for posting and managing job openings.
